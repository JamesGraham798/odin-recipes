Spicy Chicken Ramen

This Spicy Chicken Ramen is a delicious and flavourful ramen that can be an amazing dinner meal!

This simple recipe can have you making spicy chicken ramen that will remind of of that ramen you had at that japanese restaurant you fondly remember.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breast
  • kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1 Tbsp unsalter butter
  • 2 Tsp seasame or vegtable oil
  • 2 Tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 3 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 4 cups rich chicken stock
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2(3 oz) packs dried ramen noodles
  • 1 Tbsp gochujang
  1. Preheat the oven to 375â„‰. Season the chicken generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt the butter in a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken, skin-side down, and cook until the skin is golden brown and releases easily from the pan, about 5-7 minutes. Flip the chicken over and cook for another 4-5 minutes, until golden.
  3. Transfer the skillet to the oven and roast for 15-20 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through. Remove from the oven, transfer the chicken to a plate, and cover with foil until ready to serve.
  4. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat until shimmering. Add the ginger, and cook for a few minutes until softened. Add the soy sauce and mirin, and stir to combine. Cook for another minute.
  5. Add the stock, cover, and bring to a boil. Remove the lid, and let simmer uncovered for 5 minutes. Simmer gently for another 10 minutes, and season with salt, to taste.
  6. Fill a pot with enough water to cover the eggs, and bring to a boil. Gently lower the eggs (still cold from the fridge) into the boiling water, and let simmer for 7 minutes (for a slightly-runny yolk) or 8 minutes (for a soft, but set-up yolk).
  7. Meanwhile, fill a large bowl with ice water. When the timer finishes, transfer the eggs to the ice bath to stop the cooking process. Wait at least 5 minutes, or until cool enough to handle, then carefully peel away the shell and slice in half, lengthwise. Set aside until ready to serve.
  8. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until soft, then divide the noodles into two large bowls. Add the sliced chicken and the ramen broth. Top with the soft boiled egg. Serve immediately. Enjoy!!
