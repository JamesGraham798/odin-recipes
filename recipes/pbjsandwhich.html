PB and J Sandwhich

Easy Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

This is a classical PB and J Sanwhich.

You can never go wrong with this simple yet delicious snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (of any kind, your preference!)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry or Blueberry Jelly

Steps

  1. If you would like to have it toasted, throw in toaster, taking it out when complete
  2. Spread the Jelly on one side, and the peanut on the other
  3. Put both sides together and enjoy this amazing iconic snack!
